What types of dents can you fix?

We can repair most small to medium dents, door dings, and hail damage as long as the paint is not cracked and the metal is not excessively stretched. A quick in-person or photo estimate confirms if paintless dent repair is the right fit.

Not sure if your damage qualifies? Send clear photos of the dent from different angles or stop by the shop, and we will let you know whether PDR makes sense before you commit to anything.

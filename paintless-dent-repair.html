What Is Paintless Dent Repair?

Instead of sanding, filling, and repainting damaged panels, paintless dent repair uses specialized tools to gently push the metal back into its original shape from behind - no filler, no color-matching, no risk of overspray. Your factory paint stays completely intact.

Why It Matters

Oklahoma City gets hit hard - hail storms, highway debris, and tight parking lots are a fact of life here. Traditional body shop repairs can mean weeks of downtime and a repaint that never quite matches. PDR restores most hail dents and everyday damage faster, at a lower cost, and without touching your original finish. That matters for resale value, lease returns, and your own peace of mind.

Who Paintless Dent Repair Benefits

PDR is ideal for:

  • Drivers dealing with hail damage on the hood, roof, or trunk
  • Anyone with door dings or parking lot dents on a daily driver
  • Owners of newer or high-value vehicles protecting resale value
  • Leased vehicles that need to be returned in like-new condition
  • Work trucks and fleet vehicles that can't afford long shop downtime

When PDR Is the Right Choice

Most small- to medium-size dents with intact paint are excellent PDR candidates. If your paint is cracked, the edge of a panel is badly creased, or damage falls on a difficult body line, we'll tell you honestly whether PDR alone will do the job - or whether a different repair path makes more sense. No upselling, no surprises.
